Welcome to ... From the Core!

The MSVC Core Services team is part of the Nexus team in IP Development. In order to understand MSVC Core Services, it is important to understand the meaning of a "service". Cerner defines "service" as a name registered with the message bus that uniquely identifies a unit capable of processing requests. Typically, one service can process one or more requests. A service should not be confused with a server. Services are abstract in the sense that they specify a logical unit of work regardless of where the work is actually performed. Servers are the physical entities that implement the logical unit of work associated with a service.
